Installing LiveChat in Drupal:

1. Upload `livechat` directory to `/sites/all/modules/`.
2. Open your Drupal website.
3. Go to "Administration > Modules" and activate `LiveChat` module (under `Other` category).
4. Go to "Administration > > LiveChat" and follow the instructions.
